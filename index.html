Description

Blackpink restaurant is a restaurant that was founded by one of the content creators from Indonesia, Misellia Ikwan on April 4, 2019. The initial idea of ​​establishing a blackpink restaurant by Misellia was because there were so many K-POP fans in Indonesia, especially fans of the idol group Korea Blackpink. So Misellia took the opportunity to establish a Korean-themed restaurant serving Korean food and taking the blackpink theme to attract the attention of K-POP fans.
